The case for UK Nature Carbon offsetting

CARBON OFFSETTING IS CRITICAL TO REACH NET ZERO

In order to reach net zero targets, UK corporates will have to work to reduce their emissions as much as possible. Some emissions, however, will be hard to abate. For those emissions that can’t be avoided the carbon must be offset.

The current path of unit availability in the UK will not be enough to satisfy the demand, so blockers must be removed for carbon projects to reach their full potential.
