Our mission

Our Values

together we are powerful

We believe we are strongest when we work together. Because of this, we aim to unite the entire UK Nature Carbon space to accelerate climate action.

only promote real change

We only want to support projects that will generate real change. The fight against climate change is urgent – we don’t have time for greenwashing.

commitment to our community

UK Nature Carbon is nothing without the many brilliant people and businesses operating in the space, so we will always do good by them.

our founding story

The idea for Kana was born when Andy – one of our founders – wanted to make his cycle helmet business Hedkayse carbon neutral.

He hoped to offset their emissions locally rather than funding projects overseas, but after he began his search he realised that not only was it difficult to find units available for sale, but that there was no central place to source information about the projects, the project developers and their credentials.

After an arduous search he ended up on a hill in the north of Scotland with peatland project developer Caledoniam Climate, examining the project site and the impact his investment would make.

This experience was invaluable, but the effort to get there was burdensome. This got him thinking – there must be an easier way.
